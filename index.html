Find your alien friend

Find your lost friend in space. Transmit scanning signals to sense your environment and reveal your friend again. But beware: There are also hostile creatures around which will attack you when they receive your signal. Don't get caught in the blast when they fire!

Use cursor keys to move around, spacebar to scan for your friend and enemies, F/R to change the width and range of your scanning cone

Pressing R in end screens will restart the game
